What does this AQI mean?

Air Quality Index (AQI) shows how clean or polluted the air is. A higher AQI value means poorer air quality and higher health risk.

Health Tips

If AQI is below 50, the air quality is good and it is safe to do outdoor activities.

If AQI is between 51 and 100, the air quality is moderate. People with breathing problems should be careful.

If AQI is above 100, the air quality is unhealthy. Avoid long outdoor activities and stay indoors as much as possible.

Always wear a mask and drink enough water when air quality is poor.
